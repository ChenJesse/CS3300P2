What is Fiber Optics?

Fiber optic cables use glass (or plastic) threads (fibers) to transmit data. These threads are capable of transmitting messages modulated onto light waves. Fiber optics is a particularly popular technology for local-area networks and many telephone companies have begun to adopt it in place of traditional telephone lines.

What is Internet Penetration?

Internet penetration is the relationship between the number of Internet users in a certain county and its demographic data. This means the proportion of the population that has access to Internet. In the world map shown below, the countries with a darker purple shading have a greater Internet penetration.

Our world map is the main interactive feature of the website. There are various levels of data embedded throughout the map.
